2. Business Logic & Pricing Strategy

Core Pricing Principle

Final Price = Base Price √ó Demand Factor √ó Supply Factor √ó Event Factor

üìä The Three Pillars of Dynamic Pricing

üéØ Pillar 1: Demand Multiplier (0.70x - 1.30x)

What it does: Adjusts price based on predicted customer demand

  • High Demand (> 130%): Price increases up to +30% (1.30x multiplier)
  • Normal Demand (90-130%): Base price maintained (1.00x)
  • Low Demand (< 90%): Price decreases up to -30% (0.70x multiplier)

Example: If we predict 180 bookings/day vs average 100, we apply 1.25x multiplier (+25% premium)

üöó Pillar 2: Supply (Fleet Utilization) Multiplier (0.85x - 1.15x)

What it does: Adjusts price based on how many vehicles are available

  • High Utilization (> 80% rented): Price increases up to +15% (scarce supply)
  • Medium Utilization (50-80%): Base price maintained
  • Low Utilization (< 50%): Price decreases up to -15% (encourage bookings)

Example: If 90 out of 100 cars are rented (90% utilization), we apply 1.15x multiplier

üìÖ Pillar 3: Event Multiplier (1.00x - 2.00x)

What it does: Adjusts price based on special events and seasons

Umrah Season Mecca/Medina: +20% 1.20x multiplier

Location Premiums

  • Airport Locations: +10% premium (convenience factor)
  • Pre-Event Surge: 2 days before major events +15-20%
  • Weekends: +5% premium (Friday-Saturday in KSA)

5. Machine Learning Model Explained

What is Machine Learning?

Simple Explanation: Instead of writing rules like "If Friday then add 10%", the system learns patterns from historical data.

It's like having an expert analyst who has studied 2.88 years of rental data (2.48 million contracts) and can predict: "Based on similar situations in the past, tomorrow we'll likely have 180 SUV bookings in Riyadh"

Model Details

XGBoost Regression Model

  • Type: Gradient Boosted Decision Trees (XGBoost)
  • Training Data: 2.88 years of rental history (Jan 2023 - Nov 2025, 2.48M contracts)
  • Prediction Target: Number of bookings per day per category per branch
  • Accuracy: 95.63% (R¬≤ score on test set)
  • Average Error: ¬±18.8 bookings per day (RMSE: 32.5)
  • Update Frequency: Model retrained monthly with latest data

8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Does the system change prices automatically?
No. The system provides price recommendations. Branch managers review and approve prices through the dashboard before they're applied. This ensures human oversight and allows for manual adjustments if needed.
Q2: How often are prices updated?
The system can calculate prices in real-time. Typically, branch managers review prices daily or when market conditions change (new events, competitor changes, utilization shifts).
Q3: What if the prediction is wrong?
With 95.4% accuracy, predictions are very reliable. However, the system includes safeguards:
  • Multipliers are capped (max 2.00x, min 0.70x) to prevent extreme prices
  • Manager can override any recommendation
  • Model is retrained monthly with latest data to improve accuracy
Q4: How do we handle Hajj and Ramadan pricing?
The system has built-in knowledge of Islamic calendar events:
  • Hajj: Automatic +45% premium in Mecca, +20% in other cities
  • Umrah: +20% premium in Mecca and Medina during peak seasons
  • Ramadan: +15% premium nationwide (increased family travel)
  • Pre-event surge: Prices start increasing 2 days before major events
These are automatically detected and factored into pricing recommendations.
Q5: Can we adjust the pricing rules?
Yes. All pricing rules and multipliers are configurable:
  • Change event premiums (e.g., increase Hajj premium from 45% to 50%)
  • Adjust demand thresholds
  • Modify utilization impact
  • Add new event types
Changes can be made in the pricing rules configuration file and applied immediately.
Q6: What happens if the database goes down?
The system includes fallback mechanisms:
  • Uses cached data from last successful query
  • Applies base prices with conservative multipliers
  • Logs errors for investigation
  • Alerts are sent to technical team
Business operations continue uninterrupted.
Q7: How do we train staff to use the system?
The dashboard is designed for simplicity:
  • No technical knowledge required - intuitive interface
  • Clear explanations - each price shows why it's recommended
  • Competitor comparison - easy to understand competitive position
  • 15-minute training - staff can start using immediately
Training documentation and video guides are provided.
Q8: Can we see historical pricing decisions?
Yes. The system logs:
  • All pricing recommendations
  • Manager decisions (accepted/rejected)
  • Actual vs predicted demand
  • Revenue impact analysis
Monthly reports show system performance and ROI.
Q9: How accurate is the competitor data?
Competitor prices are:
  • Updated regularly (currently manual, can be automated)
  • Verified from competitor websites
  • Date-stamped for transparency
  • Filtered by branch and category for accuracy
Dashboard shows when competitor data was last updated.
Q10: What's the expected ROI timeline?
Projected ROI: (based on industry benchmarks)
  • Month 1-2: System learning and optimization
  • Month 3-6: 10-15% revenue improvement from better pricing (projected)
  • Month 6+: 15-25% sustained revenue improvement (industry average)
  • Additional benefits: Time savings, competitive intelligence, data insights

Note: These are projected improvements based on similar implementations. Actual results will be measured and reported monthly.
