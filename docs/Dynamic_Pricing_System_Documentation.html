‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often do prices update?

A: Prices update in real-time whenever branch, date, or event selections change. Competitor prices cache for 5 minutes for performance.

Q: Can managers override the ML suggestions?

A: Yes. The dashboard provides recommended prices. Final approval and overrides remain with branch managers.

Q: How accurate is the demand prediction?

A: The model achieves 95.63% R¬≤ score with average error of 32.49 bookings (on average of 332 bookings). This is highly accurate for business decisions.

Q: What happens during system downtime?

A: The system uses cached prices and falls back to rule-based pricing. Historical base prices are always available.

Q: How do we validate pricing accuracy?

A: Compare recommended prices with actual bookings and revenue. System provides downloadable reports for analysis.
